about dr. dabhade

Expert Orthopedic Surgeon in Mumbai

Dr. Mayur N. Dabhade is a highly experienced Consultant Orthopaedic Joint Replacement and Arthroscopy Surgeon with 14 years of expertise in treating a wide range of Orthopedic conditions. He holds an MBBS, a specialization in Orthopaedics (S. Ortho), a Fellowship in Joint Replacement (FIJR), and training in Pediatric & Preventive Cardiology from Sydney (IPPC).

Dr. Dabhade specializes in Orthopedic trauma, Joint Replacement Surgeries, and Arthroscopy, offering advanced solutions for conditions related to the joints and musculoskeletal system. His patient-centered approach ensures personalized treatment to restore mobility and enhance quality of life.
